Seminar on Open Source Evolution 2013

SOS Evol 2013 is an informal international meeting gathering PhD students, researchers and practitioners interested in studying the evolution of libre and open source software. The event is organised under the sponsorship of the graduate school in computing science GRASCOMP. Upon request, doctoral students can benefit from a reimbursement of their travel expenses.
