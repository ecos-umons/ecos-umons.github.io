SOS - Evol 2011 : Seminar on Open Source Evolution

Gregorio Robles

Gregorio Robles is Associate Professor at the Universidad Rey Juan Carlos (Madrid, Spain). His research interests lie in the field of empirical software engineering, especially focused on libre software, and in the area of the use of technology in education.

Evolution of a software library

Abstract of talk. Software libraries have constraints that differ from software programs. Because of this, their evolution is also different. In this talk, I want to point out some preliminary results of studying the GTK+ library and provide some points of discussion of my current research.
